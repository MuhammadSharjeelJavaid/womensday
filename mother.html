A Letter of Reverence

To Mama Ji

Mama Ji, when I opened my eyes for the very first time, yours was the first face my world ever knew. Since then, I have understood why Allah lets us witness a glimpse of divine mercy through a mother's love. I believed this truth because of the boundless love you poured into every season of my life.

You raised us with tenderness and strength, and somehow taught us to grow beyond ourselves while still belonging to your heart. Your sleepless nights, your spoken and unspoken sacrifices, and your quiet endurance mean even more to me now, as I walk toward parenthood and begin to understand the depth of what you carried for us.

A mother is one of the universe's most miraculous responsibilities, and you have held that crown with unmatched grace. In your care, love has always felt both noble and infinite.

With love and everlasting gratitude, your son.
