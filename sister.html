A Letter of Admiration

To Sidra

Sidra, when I first became self-aware, I quickly realized that half of my childhood was already set in the right direction, because all I had to do was follow your footsteps. You set the standard for discipline, excellence, and ambition in almost every walk of life.

From slam books and my earliest drawings to standing firm for what I believe is right, and facing the world with courage and relentless resolve, your influence is woven into who I am. I may look like Ghous, whose favorite personality was Rameeka, but credit belongs where it is truly due.

Even through your own upheavals, you kept reminding us of our responsibilities and anchored us to serve the family. You are an iron lady with a heart of gold. Some may notice the iron fist first, but those who know you understand the warmth and generosity that live beneath it.

Ee to manu hai.
